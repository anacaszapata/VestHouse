Our beginning

Our Beginnings (2023): October 2023: Conception of the idea for the 'RIWI' project at the university. November 2023: Initial research on the real estate investment market and development of a business plan.
First Sale with Total Transparency and Professionalism: December 2023: Launch of the 'RIWI' real estate investment platform in a pilot phase. January 2024: Completion of the first sale of a real estate fraction with a focus on transparency and professionalism.
Our First $1,000 in Real Estate Fraction Sales (March 2024): March 2024: We reached the milestone of $1,000 in real estate fraction sales, marking a significant achievement in our growth.
Who We Are (Ana Sofia Castrillón and Maria Camila Sepulveda): Throughout the process, Ana Sofia Castrillón and Maria Camila Sepulveda led the 'RIWI' project at their university. Both founders contributed their knowledge and skills to the development of the platform and in educating about the importance of creating a real estate investment portfolio.
